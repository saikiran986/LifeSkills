# Prevention of Sexual Harassment:

#### 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

* **Verbal:** Sexual jokes, comments about someone's body or clothes, asking someone out again and again after they said "No".
* **Visual:** Showing or sending inappropriate photos, videos, cartoons, or messages.
* **Physical:** Unwanted touching, hugging, standing too close, blocking someone's way, or staring in an uncomfortable way.   

#### 2. What should you do if you face or witness it?

* **Say "No" clearly:** If you feel safe, tell the person to stop and let them know their behavior is not good.
* **Keep a record:** Write down what happened, when it happened, where it happened.
* **Report it:** Inform your HR team or the company's Internal Committee. If the person is your manager, report it to higher management.
* **Support others:** If you see someone being harassed, don't ignore it. Report it or help the person report it.
