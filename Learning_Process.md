#### 3.What are active and diffused modes of thinking?

**Focus Mode** is a concentrated state used for solving problems with familiar concepts and established patterns.

**Diffuse Mode** is a relaxed, resting state that allows your brain to explore new ideas and form creative, unconventional connections.

#### 4.what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic? Only mention the points.

**1.Deconstruct the skill:** Break the skill down into smaller, more manageable pieces and prioritize the most important ones.

**2.Learn enough to self-correct:** Gather just enough resources to allow you to identify and correct your own mistakes as you practice.

**3.Remove barriers to practice:** Eliminate distractions that prevent you from sitting down to work.

**4.Practice for at least 20 hours:** 20 hours of focused effort to overcome the initial frustration barrier of feeling incompetent.
