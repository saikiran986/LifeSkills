# Listening and Active Communication

#### 1. What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening?

    - Avoid distractions.
    - Focus on the speaker.
    - Do not interrupt the speaker while they are talking.
    - Let them finish  their thought completely before you provide a response.
    - Use door openers:
            - I'm listening.
            - That sounds interesting!
            - Go ahead.
    - Write down important details during conversations.

#### 2. According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening?

    - Listen more, talk less.
    - Say back what you heard to double-check understanding.
    - See their point of view.
    - Pay attention to the feelings behind the speaker's words.

#### 3. What are the obstacles in your listening process?

    - Sometimes I get distracted.
    - Occasionally I lose focus during long discussions.

#### 4. What can you do to improve your listening?

    - Give full attention to the speaker.
    - Ask clarifying questions.
    - Listen completely before responding.
    - Ask questions for better understanding.

#### 5. When do you switch to Passive communication style in your day to day life?

    - When I am in a new environment.
    - When I want to understand others before sharing my opinion.
    - When I want to avoid conflict.

#### 6. When do you switch into Aggressive communication styles in your day to day life?

    - I generally try to avoid aggressive communication and prefer to stay calm.

#### 7. When do you switch into Passive Aggressive communication styles in your day to day life?

    - I try not to use passive-aggressive communication.
    - If I am upset, I prefer to take some time to calm down and understand the situation better.

#### 8. How can you make your communication assertive?

    - Say what you think clearly.
    - Listen to others carefully.
    - Be honest about my needs and opinions.
